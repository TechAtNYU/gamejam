Tech@NYU is hosting a game jam, a hackathon focused on game development. We are inviting students from all over New York City to explore digital and physical game design, meet new people, express themselves creatively, and bring an idea to fruition! Lunch and dinner will be served at the event.

Title Workshop Speaker Room
JavaScript as Play Phaser is a JavaScript library that allows you to create desktop and mobile games easier and faster. Learn how to make a simple platformer while also learning version control and auto deployment to azure so you can test and play your game throughout all stages of development. Rachel White is a self-taught programmer and is currently a Technical Evangelist at Microsoft. She is currently working on multiple video game projects, a VR cat cafe, and thinking about what IoT devices she can build for her two black cats. Her other interests include glitch art, 80s horror, and indie games. Broadway 5202
Breaking into Gaming with Unity Have you ever wanted to create your own games, but don’t know where to start? In this workshop we’ll teach you everything you need to know to create your first game using the popular Unity game engine. No experience required! Adina Shanholtz (aka the Fey Technologist) is a Technical Evangelist for Microsoft. She graduated from Oberlin College with a double major in Computer Science and East Asian Studies. She has a passion for Game Development, Internet of Things/Wearable technology, and empowering others through programming. In her spare time Adina likes to knit, practice her Japanese, and perform hoop and poi circus arts. Majestic 5323
3D modelling Considering the range of assets used in games today, it is impossible to overstate how important the ability to create your own models is. If you are interested in molding your own characters and such this is a workshop for you! Musegames is an independent game studio based in New York City! They are passionate about making games and are excited to share their knowledge at the Game Jam. Majestic 5323
ready.io This workshop is perfect for those with no prior experience and want to delve into the logic behind games? Ready.Io is a friendly software that promotes learning computer logic and game design all at once! Imperial 5306
